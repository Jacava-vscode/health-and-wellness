# Health & Wellness Website - Monetization Strategy Document

## Overview
This document outlines comprehensive strategies to monetize the Health & Wellness website while maintaining user trust and providing quality content.

---

## 1. AFFILIATE MARKETING

### Primary Affiliate Programs
1. **Amazon Associates**
   - Products: Supplements, fitness equipment, health books
   - Commission Rate: 1-10% (varies by product category)
   - Estimated Monthly Income: $200-$1,000+ (depends on traffic)
   - Link Format: Use tracking tags for analytics

2. **Affiliate Networks**
   - ShareASale
   - CJ Affiliate
   - Impact.com
   - Rakuten Advertising

### Implementation Tips
- Write honest product reviews before linking
- Use disclosure statements: "We may earn commission from purchases"
- Place affiliate links naturally within content
- Track which products generate most conversions
- Test different product recommendations

### Expected Performance
- Conversion Rate: 1-5% of visitors
- Average Order Value: $30-$100
- Monthly Revenue Potential: $300-$2,000 (with 10,000 visitors)

---

## 2. GOOGLE ADSENSE

### Setup
1. Apply for Google AdSense
2. Add ad code to HTML pages
3. Optimize ad placement for user experience
4. Monitor performance in AdSense dashboard

### Ad Placement Strategies
- **Above the Fold**: High visibility, high CPM
- **Within Content**: Natural integration
- **Sidebar**: Secondary placements
- **Footer**: Minimal impact on UX

### Revenue Estimates
- CPM (Cost Per Mille): $5-$15 for health/wellness content
- Monthly Revenue: $100-$1,000 (with 10,000 visitors)
- Peak Seasons: January (New Year's Resolutions), June (Summer Body Goals)

## 10. TRAFFIC GROWTH STRATEGIES

To maximize revenue, focus on growing traffic:

### Content Marketing
- Publish 2-4 posts/week
- Target long-tail keywords
- Create comprehensive guides
- Update old posts quarterly

### SEO Optimization
- Optimize for search intent
- Build internal linking structure
- Create content clusters
- Improve page speed

### Social Media
- Share posts on Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok
- Engage with wellness community
- Use hashtags strategically
- Collaborate with influencers

### Email Marketing
- Build list organically
- Send weekly emails
- Segment audience
- Test different subject lines

### Paid Advertising (if budget allows)
- Google Ads: $1,000+ minimum
- Facebook/Instagram Ads: $5-$10/day
- Pinterest Ads: $5+/day
- ROI Target: 3:1 or better
