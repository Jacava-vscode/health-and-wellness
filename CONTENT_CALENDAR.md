# Health & Wellness Content Calendar & Strategy Guide

## 12-Month Content Strategy

### Overview
This calendar outlines a comprehensive content strategy designed to:
- Build topical authority
- Improve search rankings
- Engage audience
- Drive monetization opportunities
- Establish thought leadership

## CONTENT GAPS TO ADDRESS

### Audience Research Questions
- What health topics interest your audience most?
- What problems are they trying to solve?
- What content do competitors lack?
- What seasonal topics matter?

### Keyword Gap Analysis
1. Identify 100+ long-tail keywords in your niche
2. Find keywords with decent search volume (100-1000/month)
3. Prioritize low-competition keywords (KD < 30)
4. Create content for top 50 keywords in first year

## CONTENT PERFORMANCE TRACKING

### Metrics to Monitor
- Page views (target: +20% monthly)
- Time on page (target: 3+ minutes)
- Bounce rate (target: <50%)
- Click-through rate (CTR) to affiliate links
- Email signups from content
- Social shares
- Backlinks earned

### Monthly Content Audit
- Top 5 performing articles (analyze why)
- Bottom 5 articles (improve or rewrite)
- Keywords with ranking potential
- Content gaps discovered
- Competitor analysis updates

## Final Notes

- **Consistency is key**: Publish regularly, even if it's just 1-2x per week
- **Quality over quantity**: 10 excellent articles beat 30 mediocre ones
- **SEO-first approach**: Write for search engines AND humans
- **Track everything**: What gets measured gets improved
- **Be patient**: Authority takes 6-12+ months to build
- **Adapt and adjust**: Use data to guide your strategy

**Remember**: The best content is the content you actually publish!
